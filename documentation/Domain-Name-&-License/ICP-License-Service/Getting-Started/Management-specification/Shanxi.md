# License Rules in Shaanxi

### Rules of Unit Filing in Shaanxi:

1. Enterprises must use the new documents integrating the three certificates to perform filing

2. The person in charge of an entity must be the legal person

3. If the person in charge of a website is not the legal person, please be sure to submit the [Power of Attorney for Person in Charge of Website.docx](https://badownload.s3.cn-north-1.jdcloud-oss.com/buchongziliao/shanxi/shanxishouquanshu.doc)

4. **All the reported mobile numbers should be the mobile number of Shaanxi**

5. **In case of multiple websites, the website names cannot be the same**

6. **A website without an unlicensed domain cannot be opened. Otherwise, the license will fail**

7. **For the filed website, after it is opened**, there must be the hyperlink of filing number and the mark of the ownership of copyright on the bottom of the webpage;

8. **In case of prior filing with JD**, its filing information must be consistent with the actual information of the current unit. If there is any inconsistency, please go to the original filing provider to make change, and then apply for a new one

9. To change a filed subject, a proof issued by the changed unit for the change shall be provided

10. The filing website count shall not exceed **30**

11. Prepare materials before filing:

   ① Clear color pictures of the Business License (Unified Social Credit Code)

   ② Clear color pictures of the front and back of legal person’s ID card

   ③ Clear color pictures of the front and back of website responsible person’s ID card

   ④ Official seal of the filing unit (the Verification Form shall be sealed during the process)

   ⑤ **Certificate of Domain Name (this should be provided when the cumulative quantity of subject’s filing is ≥5)**
   
   ⑥ Letter of Authorization (Provided if the person in charge of the website isn’t a legal person)
   

### Rules of Personal Filing in Shaanxi:

1. Chinese citizens must use 2nd-generation ID card for filing

2. **All the reported mobile numbers should be the mobile number of Shaanxi**

3. **In case of multiple websites, the website names cannot be the same**

4. **A website without an unlicensed domain cannot be opened. Otherwise, the license will fail**

5. **After the filed website has been opened**, there must be a hyperlink of the filing number and a mark for the copyright of the website under the webpage, **the website of an unfiled domain name shall not be opened, otherwise the filing will fail**

6. **In case of prior filing with JD**, its filing information must be consistent with the actual information of the current unit. If there is any inconsistency, please go to the original filing provider to make change, and then apply for a new one

7. Personal filing cannot be changed to other individuals or units.

8. The filing website count shall not exceed **5**

9. Prepare materials before filing:

   ① The clear color picture of the front and back of the personal ID card

   ② **Certificate of Domain Name (this should be provided when the cumulative quantity of subject’s filing is ≥5)**
