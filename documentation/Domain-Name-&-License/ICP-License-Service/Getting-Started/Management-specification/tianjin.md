# **License Rules in Tianjin**

### License Rules for Organization in Tianjin

1. Enterprises must be licensed with their business licenses

2. The person in charge of an entity must be the legal person

3. The person in charge of a website cannot be the legal person

4. A local mobile phone number is required for license

5. The emergency telephone number cannot the same as those of the person in charge of an entity and the person in charge of a website, and thus a third-party mobile phone number is required

6. When the licensed subject is to be changed, the certificate of change of the change unit must be provided

7. Materials that should be prepared before license:

   ① Clear color image of the business license with a unified social credit code

   ② Clear color images of both sides of the ID card of the legal person

   ③ Clear color images of both sides of the ID card of the person in charge of website

   ④ Official seal of the license unit to stamp on the verification form in the process

   

### License Rules for Individual in Tianjin:

1. Chinese citizens must use the 2nd-generation ID card for license, and anyone under 18 is not allowed to apply for a license

2. A local mobile phone number is required for license

3. The emergency telephone number should be the phone number of an immediate relative of the website owner, and XXX (the user of the emergency telephone number) and XX (the family relationship with the website owner) shall be filled in remarks

4. Licensed entities cannot be changed to other individuals or organizations

5. Material preparation before license: clear color images of ID card (both sides)
