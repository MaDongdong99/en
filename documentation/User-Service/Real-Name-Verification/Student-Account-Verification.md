# Student Verification Process
## I. Users who have already passed JD Campus Baitiao student verification
If your student verification has been approved by the Campus Baitiao of JD, you can authorize JD Cloud to collect your student verification information.
1. Click ***Authorize Now* in Account Management-Student Verification to authorize student verification information;
![](../../../image/User/Real%20name%20verification/Student%20account%20verification/%E5%AD%A6%E7%94%9F%E8%AE%A4%E8%AF%81-%E7%AB%8B%E5%8D%B3%E6%8E%88%E6%9D%83.jpg)

2. When the authorization is done, the notification that you have passed the student verification of JD Cloud will be given to you.
![](../../../image/User/Real%20name%20verification/Student%20account%20verification/%E6%A0%A1%E5%9B%AD%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8%E4%B8%93%E5%8C%BA.jpg)
